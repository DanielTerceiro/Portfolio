About Me

UI / UX Designer - Graphic Designer - Front End Dev

I am Daniel Terceiro, a seasoned Creative Designer and UX/UI Specialist with over 10 years of dedicated experience. My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Design and Applied Arts, followed by a Professional Diploma in UX Design from the UX Design Institute.

My primary focus is on UX/UI design, where I have made impactful contributions to high-profile projects. From crafting compelling marketing materials for web, print, and in-game experiences to collaborating closely with Art Directors on concept cover art.

I have a passion for creating user interfaces that are customer-focused, intuitive and aesthetic. I enjoy tackling complex problems, designing engaging experiences, and growing the business by delivering what customers want.
